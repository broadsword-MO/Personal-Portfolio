Profile

I'm Doug Wallace and I'm working toward being a front-end developer. I live on an off-grid, solar powered, homestead-in-progress way back in the hills of the Missouri Ozarks with my wife, Amanda, and our children. Over the last two years, as time has permitted, I've been going through freeCodeCamp's wonderful curriculum on HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This has been supplemented with lots of other material from various authors of which the Kevin Powell YouTube videos, the Per Harald Borgen Scrimba videos and the Syntax podcast with Scott Tolinski and Wes Bos are my current favorites.
